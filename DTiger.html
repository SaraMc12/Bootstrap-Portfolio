More about PBS kids Promo- Miss Elaina

The PBS kids spotlight videos were created to showcase women and minority characters as an effort to creat more inclusion to PBS's young audience. Miss Elaina was a fun character to write a promo for. When I worked on the project there was no where near as many shows to choose clips from as there was with Arthur since Daniel Tiger was still a new show. However, I saw the potential right away in making a promo which highlighted her intelligence and positive attitude.

What was both fun and challenging about both promos was learning how to create a video script using clips from a show that went along with my VO script. This meant I had to learn the art of "Cherry Picking" as well as having both scripts had to be under 45 seconds.
