About Sara

Hello, I am a Maryland native that is happy to now call Chicago my home. I have worked as a creative consultant for the last five years, and before that I was in television production. When I first moved to Chicago, I was pleased to have to opportunity to work at The Second City where I took away a fresh outlook on teamwork and how knowing when someone has your back cultivates true innovation and creative problem solving.

With those new insights in my toolbelt I moved forward as an independent creative consultant and illustrated two children’s books. I love to learn and never want to stop growing as a person. I decided to move into the world of web development after I created some mockups and wireframes for a friend’s website. After that I was hooked on learning more which led her to enrolling in Northwestern coding bootcamp. I hope one day to use my skills to help the environment and work with AI.
