More About PBS Kids Promo -DW

The PBS kids spotlight videos were created to showcase mainly women and minority characters. I was first tasked with a show that was familiar to me as I grew up watching it myself. What was both fun and challenging about this project was that I had always known DW as “the bratty sister” and in this particular promo PBS wanted to highlight her in a positive way.

I utilized my editing skills and writing skills to recreate a DW that, in the past might have been considered perhaps a bit troublesome into brave, independent girl who tells it like it is and never backs down from a new adventure or challenge. I was glad I was able to work on this project as I do feel like young girls need to have early introductions to strong female characters and DW definitely did fit that role and I was glad PBS saw it too.
